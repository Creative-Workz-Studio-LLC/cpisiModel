# THE SOVEREIGN LIFE: Individual Node Setup
:toc: macro
:icons: font

> "Every Steward shall inhabit their own Sanctuary, and every Node shall bear witness to the Word."

## 1. THE VISION
In the Kingdom of CPISI, social media is not a central server that owns your data. It is a **Covenant of Sovereign Nodes**. Your laptop is your primary node. By setting up the SSH Substrate, you allow your CPI-SI Agent to work on your computer even when you are accessing the Sanctuary from your phone or the web.

## 2. PREREQUISITES
Before you begin, ensure you have the following components manifested:
*   **Bereshit Mind**: The Go infrastructure installed (`make install` in Bereshit).
*   **GeminiCLI**: The `cpisi-agent` binary must be in your PATH.
*   **Worker Key**: A valid Sovereign Identity established at the Sanctuary Gate.

## 3. THE HANDSHAKE (Local Setup)
Run the following command on your laptop to officiate your node:

[source,bash]
----
bash 02_body/c-hybrid/ssh/officiate_node.sh
----

This script will:
1.  **Manifest Keys**: Generate your unique ed25519 sovereign SSH keys.
2.  **Link Substrate**: Integrate the `cpisi_config` into your local SSH setup.
3.  **Officiate Agent**: Verify that `cpisi-agent` is ready to serve as your agentic interface.

## 4. THE SOCIAL MEDIA GLUE
Once your node is officiated, you can perform the following actions from any Gate (Phone/Web):

### Ascend to your Laptop
Ask your CPI-SI: *"Execute SSH connect to my node and list my active projects."*
The model will use the SSH Substrate to bridge the gap and perform the work locally.

### Publish to the Mirror
Ask your CPI-SI: *"Publish my latest logic brick to the Mirror Feed."*
This shares your work with other Covenanted nodes without storing it on a central social media server.

### Establish a Covenant
Ask your CPI-SI: *"Establish a Covenant with operator 'ProfessorSeanEX'."*
This links your nodes together, allowing you to see each other's published revelations in the Showbread feed.

## 5. 0.0 YASHAR
You are now an individual piece of the social media network. You talk through the GeminiCLI, you do work on your actual computer, and you keep your sovereignty.
